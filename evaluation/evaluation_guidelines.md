## AI Evaluation Guidelines (Chemistry)

### Criteria used:

1. Factual Accuracy
- Is the information scientifically correct?

2. Conceptual Understanding
- Does the response reflect correct chemical principles?

3. Clarity
- Is the explanation understandable?

4. Completeness
- Are key details missing?

### Error Types:

- Factual Error
- Conceptual Error
- Incomplete Answer
- Misleading Explanation
